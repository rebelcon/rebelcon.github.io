19th & 20th JUNE 2019
The Clayton Hotel, Cork City

Felicity Neary
Ensure Success: Prototyping 101

How can we ensure the success of our products prior to release? A business idea is only powerful if it has been validated. In this session, attendees will learn the basics of prototyping and how, by building early concepts, you can ensure its usability. Audience members will leave with an understanding of the prototyping tools available as well as how to conduct basic user testing. Applying these techniques to their feature ideas, they will then be able to build the correct product for their users.

Felicity Neary

UX Product Manager at Workday

Felicity is a User Experience Product Manager. When she is not crafting user workflows during the day, she is spreading the knowledge of the latest practices of user-centred product development by night. Felicity has a unique view of the development process having been a product designer prior to making the move to product management. Her main passion is aligning developers, designers and leadership in order to build features that users love. In her spare time, you may find Felicity practicing yoga, rock climbing or on stage in a musical.
